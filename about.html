About me

I'm Joseph Cooper, a 20 year old multidisciplinary designer based on the outskirts of London. Currently I am in my final year at the University of Reading studying Graphic Communications. Whilst studying I take on freelance work and last year I also worked part-time as a digital product designer for Loot.

I found myself designing from the age of 13 by teaching myself programs like Adobe Photoshop, I quickly fell in love with design. Since then I have worked with a range of clients working on all areas of design (and occasionally code). My specialty is in UI and UX.

If you have a project I can help with, or just want to talk, feel free to email me: Joseph@cooperland.co.uk
